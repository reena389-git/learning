CCR analytics layer — rough-draft diagram

Boxes only · shows dimensions sourced from domains, facts sourced from Risk apps, dimensional layer marrying them, flat consumption fact for BI · CCR worked example, but pattern scales to LRM/MR/Capital
How to read this diagram: Reading bottom-to-top: Tier 1 source systems (Risk apps producing measures + Domain data products providing entities) feed into Tier 2 Silver per-domain LDMs (our CCR/Collateral work + future LRM, Capital, XVA, Market Risk Silver layers). Silver feeds into Tier 3 dimensional layer (subject-level facts on the left, conformed dimensions on the right). The dimensional layer is the marriage point — facts join to conformed dims. Tier 4 consumption surfaces this through a flat denormalized view (or directly on the star schemas) into BI tools (PowerBI, MicroStrategy).

Key principles encoded in the diagram: (1) Conformed dimensions are shared across facts. (2) Each dimension's source domain is named. (3) Each fact's producing Risk app is named. (4) Adjacent / future domains are dashed.
